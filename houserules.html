House Rules & Regulations

One-Bedroom Apartment

Welcome to Vinu's Place. To guarantee an exceptional standard of accommodation and ensure mutual respect for the property and surrounding residents, guests are required to strictly adhere to the following rules during their occupancy.

1. Check-In and Check-Out Schedule
Standard check-in is scheduled for 14:00 onwards. Check-out must be completed by 11:00. Requests for alterations to these parameters must be submitted and approved in writing prior to arrival.
2. Maximum Occupancy Policy
The maximum allowable overnight occupancy for this premises is limited to two registered guests. Unregistered visitors are not permitted on the property without prior authorization from management.
3. Smoking Restrictions
This is a strictly smoke-free and vape-free facility. Smoking is completely prohibited anywhere inside the interior perimeter of the apartment. Designated outdoor spaces may be utilized, provided external doors remain closed.
4. Noise Regulations and Quiet Hours
To preserve localized structural harmony, quiet hours are legally mandated from 22:00 until 07:00. Sound systems, televisions, and general dialogue must be managed at a non-disruptive volume level.
5. Pet Regulations
No animals or biological pets are permitted within the apartment boundaries at any time, protecting the sanitization and allergen-free profile of the space.
6. Resource and Energy Conservation
Guests are required to deactivate climate control systems, interior illumination, and electronic appliances when vacating the property for external excursions.
7. Property Maintenance and Waste Management
The premises must be maintained in a clean condition. All culinary implements must be washed after use, and accumulated refuse must be correctly deposited into the designated waste containers provided.
8. Key Liability and Property Damage
Any structural anomalies, breakages, or operational defects must be immediately declared to management. Guests assume total financial liability for structural damage or lost master key sets.

Compliance Notice: Non-compliance with the established contractual clauses above may result in immediate revocation of the occupancy agreement, eviction without remediation, and the forfeiture of all associated deposits.
